6. FAQ

Are your products handmade?

Yes, all our products are handcrafted in small batches to ensure the highest quality and attention to detail.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept Bank Transfer, Bank Deposit, and Cash on Delivery (COD).

How long does delivery take?

Delivery usually takes 2–5 working days within Sri Lanka after payment confirmation.

Do you offer Cash on Delivery?

Yes, COD is available for selected locations. Please note that our team may contact you to confirm your order details before dispatching your package.

How long does one soap bar last & how should I store it?

Typically, a bar lasts 3–5 weeks depending on usage. To make it last longer, keep it in a dry, well-draining soap dish between uses.

Pro-Tip: Avoid letting the soap sit in a pool of water!

Are your products suitable for sensitive skin?

While we use natural ingredients, we highly recommend performing a patch test on your inner forearm before full use to ensure compatibility with your skin.
